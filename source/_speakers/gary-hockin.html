Gary Hockin has been a web developer for over 15 years. He's a valued contributor to both Zend Framework 1 and 2, in addition to being on the ZF2 documentation team. Gary is Technical Lead of Training and Certification for Zend, along with being part of Roave, the badass PHP developers. When not working or spending time with his wife and two daughters, Gary can sometimes be found at the pub playing competitive pool.
