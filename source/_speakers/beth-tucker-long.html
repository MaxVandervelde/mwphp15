Beth Tucker Long is a PHP developer and advocate at Code Climate and co-organizer of Madison PHP. You can find her on Twitter (@e3betht) or on her blog (http://www.alittleofboth.com). She runs Treeline Design, a web development company, and Playlist Event Music, a DJ company, along with her husband, Chris.
