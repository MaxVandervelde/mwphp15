Chris Tankersley is a husband, father, and PHP developer in Northwest Ohio. He works for The Brick Factory in Washington, D.C. doing Drupal, Wordpress, and custom development. He founded the Northwest Ohio PHP User Group, and works with local developers helping them with programming and server administration. He works with PHP primarily, normally with Zend Framework, Symfony, and Silex.
