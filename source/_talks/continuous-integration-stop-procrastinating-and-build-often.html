Continuous Integration (CI) is quickly becoming a de-facto standard for PHP development teams. CI allows for more frequent deployments and more ambitious refactoring, and significantly reduces the number of bugs deployed to production. Oftentimes, the biggest obstacle to implementing CI is the unknown. In this session, we will de-mystify the process and take a look at how easy it is to get a minimum viable continuous integration environment in place using Jenkins, PHPUnit, Selenium, and chat.
