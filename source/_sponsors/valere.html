In 2006, Sandy Dischinger and Deb Heuer founded VALERE (vaˈlere) with the firm belief that why and how you do business is as important as what you deliver. With over 20 years of corporate experience, they knew that it was possible to deliver exceptional talent and services to clients while maintaining high standards of business practice, work relationships, and corporate citizenship. The Latin term valere — “strength,” “worthiness” and “health”—expresses the principles that define the culture clients and consultants experience when working with VALERE:

  • Respectful relationships
  • Open and responsive communication
  • Accountability and dependability
  • Community engagement

VALERE is located in Minnesota with consultants delivering creative and information technology services to clients in retail, healthcare, finance, advertising and e-commerce industries. We call the Twin Cities home and strive to make our community better by giving back.
